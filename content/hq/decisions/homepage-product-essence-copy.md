## Decision

Make the homepage product-row copy read as one-sentence work statements: Notes keeps the thought until it earns a task, Tasks keeps ownership clear, Timeline gives everyone one page, and Signal says what actually needs you today.

## Reason

The prior right-rail copy mixed product explanation, feature language, and slogan language. The section works harder when each row names one plain job in the day of work.

## Alternatives considered

Keep the current lines and only tune the weakest row. Move sharper and more manifesto-heavy across all four rows.

## Risks

The copy is tighter and gives fewer feature details. If a first-time reader needs more explanation, the product pages should carry that load.

## Notes

Shipped in `RevealProducts` and mirrored in suite messaging so the public surface and HQ record stay aligned. The CTA meta row now keeps the open link on one line.
