## Decision

Ethan McNamara, founder of Signal Studio, approved the Signal Experience Standard and explicitly authorized Codex to execute the remaining quality-system closeout on his behalf in the 2026-07-15 Codex task. ## Required amendments

Approval is conditioned on preserving the founder's exact score anchors: 0 Broken or absent, 1 Functional but materially deficient, 2 Competent, 3 Polished, and 4 Studio-grade. The executable gate must also fail closed when rendered evidence is missing, an audit uses an unknown or unrequired state, a duplicate audit tuple exists, a registry claims passing without a complete passing matrix, or an exception is missing, expired, unlinked, outside scope, or not founder-approved.

Those amendments are part of the approved standard. No authorization in this receipt permits an agent to invent evidence, approve a failing candidate, bypass a hard blocker, expose credentials, or describe incomplete suite coverage as certified.

## Evidence rule for delegated approvals

The founder's authorization may be applied to a golden reference or visual baseline only after the exact candidate hash, deterministic capture result, review record, rendered artifact, and required 13-dimension scores exist and pass. Each resulting approval record must name Ethan McNamara as approver, cite this receipt, and preserve any broader unaudited scope as incomplete.
