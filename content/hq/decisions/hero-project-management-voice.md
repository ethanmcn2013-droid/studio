## Decision

Replace homepage H1 with: Project Management without the project-manager voice.

## Reason

BRAND.md §2 names this verbatim as a quotable target. Value-based ('what we remove') is stronger than exclusion-based ('who it isn't for'). Same length, same scannability, sharper anti-theatre register.

## Alternatives considered

Stay on 'Project management for people not in tech.' or move all the way to 'Cut through the noise.' as H1.

## Risks

Two H1 changes in 24 hours is brand-churn — relies on this one sticking. Some readers may not have an existing mental model of 'project-manager voice' the way they do of 'tech'.
