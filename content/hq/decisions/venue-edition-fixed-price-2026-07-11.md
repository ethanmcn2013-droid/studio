## Decision

Venue Edition is **€1,500 per venue, per year, prepaid**. It is one price for every venue. Venue size and number of sites do not create a higher tier. The couple never sees a price, and the venue does not manage seats.

Founding venues still lock €1,500 for as long as they stay. That lock protects the founding relationship against a future offer change; it is not a lower current price. Each sponsored couple receives 18 months of access, as confirmed by the founder on 2026-07-01.

## Reason

One number is easier to understand, quote, invoice, and defend. The old €1,500–€4,000 range introduced a negotiation before the buyer had seen proof and made the offer feel sized like enterprise software. A fixed price keeps the conversation on the outcome: the venue stands behind every couple's planning.

## Commercial effect

The simpler offer lowers the modeled annual value of non-founding venues. Every current forecast, lender pack, pitch deck, structured-data offer, venue asset, and new-write path must use €1,500. Existing sponsor-ledger amounts remain exact historical cash records and are not normalized.

## Risk

Revenue targets that depended on a €2,500–€3,000 blended venue price no longer hold. With volume unchanged, 18 venues produce €27,000, 60 produce €90,000, and 120 produce €180,000 in venue cash. Volume, consumer revenue, or the target must move; the price will not be padded to rescue the model.

## Implementation

The public venue page, Signal HQ, the Access onboarding writer, the operator CLI, current strategy and growth records, cross-suite references, collateral masters, rendered PDFs, and financial models must all read from this decision. Historical changelog, dispatch, and cash-ledger records remain as dated evidence of what was true before this amendment.
