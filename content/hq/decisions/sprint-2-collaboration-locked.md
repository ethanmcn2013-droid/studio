## Decision

Sprint 2 = Collaboration moat. Five gestures, seven cycles, Tasks + Timeline only.

## Reason

Collaboration is the brand's structural moat: 'the invited person feels nothing.' The five gestures (one-click invite, no-signup guest view, 'invited by X' context, plain-English activity, one-tap reply) load-bear the 'wow intuitive' feeling. Plan locked in studio/docs/SPRINT_2_COLLABORATION.md.

## Alternatives considered

Generic 'add collab everywhere' (would break Notes locked single-user and Signal private positioning). Or defer collaboration until after Signal ships (loses the moat-building window).

## Risks

Sprint 2 is multi-cycle and cross-repo. Pacing matters. Don't half-start it before Sprint 1 (Notes 9.3+9.4+9.5) is closed. Cycle 9.4 promote-to-tasks is the first collab edge — finish it before 10.1.

## Notes

Notes stays single-user, Signal stays private. Sprint 2 changes only Tasks + Timeline.
