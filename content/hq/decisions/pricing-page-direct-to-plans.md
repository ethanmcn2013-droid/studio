## Decision

Remove the grey four-product strip directly under the pricing hero so readers move from the pricing promise straight into Plans.

## Reason

People who click Pricing are asking for the price. The suite explanation is still useful, but it belongs after the plan grid where it explains what the tiers include.

## Alternatives considered

Keep the strip and tighten its copy. Move it below the plans. Remove it entirely from pricing.

## Risks

The page gives less immediate product context before the tiers. The lower "What's in Signal Studio" section carries that context.

## Notes

This keeps `/pricing` commercially direct without changing the pricing model.
