## Decision

Replace homepage H1 with: Project Management for the 80% not in tech.

## Reason

Audience-clarity over voice-claim. Names who the suite is for in the H1 itself rather than describing what the suite avoids. Reverses the 2026-05-11 value-based pivot.

## Alternatives considered

Keep 'Project Management without the project-manager voice.' (BRAND.md §2 quotable). Or 'Project Management for the 80%.' without the 'not in tech' qualifier.

## Risks

Third H1 in three days — brand churn risk is real. Headline now restates the subhead ('Built for the 80%') so subhead was rewritten to 'Built for the work, not the workflow.' to preserve hero/subhead split. 'Not in tech' framing may read as exclusionary to the broader audience archetypes in BRAND.md §2.1 (operators, coordinators) who don't think of themselves as 'not in tech.'

## Notes

Hero choreography preserved — three-line word-by-word stagger with 'em' emphasis moved from 'without' to '80%'. Subhead rewritten to avoid redundancy with new H1. SEO title + OG alt updated to match.
