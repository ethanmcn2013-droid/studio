## Decision

Not a re-strategy. The ratified frame (`BUSINESS_PARTNER_REVIEW_2026_05.md`) holds in full and is not reopened. This review records the gap between the frame and execution since 16 May, and the decisions that close it. Full analysis: `docs/strategy/STRATEGY_REVIEW_2026_05_18.md`.

**The one thing.** The frame is right; the proof gate has not moved. Since the spine was ratified: 7 UX waves, 5 re-score rounds, suite-consolidation Phases 0–3, and a new seamless-ecosystem plan — all real, none of it the five metrics. Zero founder-signed outreaches sent, zero booked calls, zero paid pilots. The 60-day kill clock has not started because the act that starts it has not happened.

**30-day plan status.** Items 1–3 (demo, Timeline example, venue landing) substantially done. Item 4 (20 founder-signed outreaches) drafted, not sent. Item 5 (five metrics) structurally zero until item 4 starts. The buildable, founder-comfortable work is complete; the unscalable, founder-only commercial work has not started.

**Decisions.** (1) Product freeze behind seamless-ecosystem Layers 0–3 only — the one demo-credibility carve-out; Layer 4, Timeline draft/publish, suite-consolidation Phase 4/5, any new surface are post-gate. (2) Outreach-start on or before 2026-05-25 (first five sent, not drafted). (3) Confirm or build the five-metric instrument as the only permitted measurement work. (4) Concrete kill clock: 20 sent by 2026-06-08, kill-clock expiry 2026-08-07, integrity checkpoint 2026-06-16.

## Reason

Every cycle since 16 May had a legitimate-sounding product reason and was individually defensible; in aggregate they consumed the entire 30-day window with zero movement on the only metric the spine says matters. The product is now world-class by the team's own rubric and not one venue has been asked to pay for it. Five of six spine risks cannot be retired by building — only by contact with a real buyer. Continued product investment does not lower portfolio risk; it raises it by burning the founder-time the spine named as the single point of failure. The seamless-ecosystem demo-credibility slice (authed user sees logged-out marketing CTA mid-demo) is the one piece of in-flight work genuinely on the commercial critical path, hence the single carve-out rather than a blanket freeze.

## Risks

Risk #6 (founder-time concentration) re-rated from "accepted, not mitigated" to "actively realising — needs a structural forcing function": product polish is functioning as productive avoidance of the founder-only act of asking a venue for money. Other five spine risks unchanged but note: buyer≠sufferer and diffuse-pain remain *untested* and can only be tested by sending outreach, not by more building. An inert kill clock (start event hasn't occurred) is the absence of a decision wearing discipline's clothes.

## Kill / pivot triggers

Spine §8 triggers unchanged and still correct. This review makes the clock concrete so it is no longer inert: outreach-start ≤ 2026-05-25; 20 sent by 2026-06-08; kill-clock expiry 2026-08-07 (60 days from full send) — if zero paid pilots and consistent stated reason is "not worth paying for" (not timing/fit), spine §8 venue-wedge kill fires and contractor-pivot evaluation opens; honest integrity checkpoint 2026-06-16 (instrument + send-cadence check, not a kill point).
