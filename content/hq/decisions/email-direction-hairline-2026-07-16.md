## Decision

Signal Studio's email design system is **Hairline**: the engraved
monochrome direction from the three-way comparison at `/hq/email-lab`
(scorecard in `docs/email-system/comparison.md`, 89/100 and the
recommendation). One rule weight, one column at 520px, mono metadata
labels, tabular figures, the 1px ink-ruled action, composed plain-text
twins, no tracking of any kind.

One founder amendment to the charter: **clickable links render in
indigo**, not ink. Hairline's indigo budget is therefore the wordmark's
dot plus its links, the same rationing grammar as the rest of the suite:
indigo means "you can act here" and nothing else. Buttons stay ink-ruled
and unfilled.

## What this settles

- Every new email is designed in Hairline first; the four-mode contract
  (utility, guided, founder, editorial) is served by one direction.
- Broadsheet and Letterhead are not deleted: their tokens and renders
  stay in the Lab as reference registers, and Letterhead remains the
  written reference for founder-letter copy structure (salutation,
  enclosure, signature), rendered through Hairline.
- The company's address line in email is Limerick. Any Dublin copy in
  mail surfaces was a mistake and is corrected.

## What this does not settle

The nine operational decisions in `docs/email-system/decisions-required.md`
(senders, DKIM, prices, Dispatch edition, verification, waitlist
confirmation approval) remain open and unchanged.
