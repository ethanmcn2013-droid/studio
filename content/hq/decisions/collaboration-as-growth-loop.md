## Decision

Treat collaboration as the organic outreach loop.

## Reason

The strongest acquisition path is clear shared work that invites collaborators, creates useful outputs, and turns some viewers into workspace creators.

## Alternatives considered

Rely mainly on public content, paid ads, or founder-led outbound.

## Risks

Poor guest experience or unclear public controls could create friction or trust risk.

## Notes

Prioritise invite value, shared artefacts, and source tracking before heavier monetisation.
