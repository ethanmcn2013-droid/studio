## Decision

Build Timeline's shared update page as the first real shareable artefact.

## Reason

The collaboration growth loop needs a useful output people can forward before invitations, templates, and signal tracking are fully built.

## Alternatives considered

Start with the wedding template, Today Signal briefing, or Notes meeting follow-up.

## Risks

The page could stay a generic timeline summary unless it becomes part of the wedding/events demo and carries source tracking.

## Notes

`/[workspace]/update` is the first built artefact. It should lead into wedding-specific sharing and view-to-creator tracking.
