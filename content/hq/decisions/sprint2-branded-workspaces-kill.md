## Decision

Kill Sprint 2 Cycle 10.6 (branded workspaces — venue logo + accent on shared view).

## Reason

BRAND.md §5 locks 'one accent across the suite, differentiation comes from gesture not colour.' Venue logo + custom accent on the shared Roadmap view is white-labeling — turns Signal Roadmap from a clarity product into a CMS competing with Webflow. The discipline moat is sustained refusal; this is the most reasonable-sounding violation worth refusing.

## Alternatives considered

Ship as a delight feature in Sprint 2. Or move to a paid-tier gesture later.

## Risks

Venues sell to couples partly on aesthetic continuity. Losing pilots over missing custom branding is a real-but-not-imminent risk.

## Notes

Hold the line through ten pilots. If five of them ask for custom branding, revisit as a paid-tier decision, not a sprint gesture.
