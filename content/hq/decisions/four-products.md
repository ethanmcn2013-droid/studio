## Decision

Keep all four products as one ecosystem.

## Reason

Each product owns one layer of clear work: context, execution, direction, and attention.

## Alternatives considered

Lead with Tasks only and defer the rest.

## Risks

The suite can feel scattered if shared objects and outputs lag behind.

## Notes

The ecosystem is the product.
