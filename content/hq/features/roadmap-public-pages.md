## Notes

This is the historical shareable-page capability. It made plans readable without requiring the recipient to create an account.

The selected production successor is `timeline-shareable-artifact`: an owner-private Workspace publishes one allowlisted artifact through an unguessable, rotatable, revocable link. “Shareable” does not mean listed, indexed, or discoverable. Keep this record as lineage; do not use it as proof that the new Option D route or qualified view count is deployed.
