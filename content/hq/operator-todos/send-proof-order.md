## Steps

1. Download `/brand/collateral/cards/proof-order-card-trio.zip` — six PDFs
   plus `PRINT-ORDER.txt`, written to be pasted into an email to any
   quality printer (spec: 350–400gsm uncoated, rich black, PMS 2726C,
   10 of each variant = 60 proof cards).
2. Recommended: a Limerick printer — the 100 Relationship Strategy says
   the printer is a relationship too.
3. When proofs arrive: check the QR scans, the black is even, the indigo
   matches across pieces — then approve the production run by touch.
4. Also carried from the venue-kit approval: permission form past a
   solicitor before its first real signature.
