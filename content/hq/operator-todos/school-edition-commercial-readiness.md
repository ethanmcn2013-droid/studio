## Steps

1. Confirm €1,500 per school per year, prepaid, for up to 60 verified staff.
2. Confirm the Year 1 product has zero pupil accounts and zero pupil personal data.
3. Obtain accountant advice on VAT, sterling invoicing, and revenue recognition for UK and Irish sales.
4. Prepare the teacher-only privacy notice, data-processing position, procurement answers, and pilot agreement.
5. Do not issue a paid school contract until these terms are approved and the product enforces the boundary.
