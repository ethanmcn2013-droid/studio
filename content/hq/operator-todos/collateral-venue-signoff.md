## Steps

1. Open `/brand/collateral/venue/index.html` — review the A4 one-pager and
   A5 leave-behind against the seven-second test: would the best venue in
   Limerick keep this on its front desk?
2. **QR destination:** both objects currently point to signalstudio.ie
   (real). If you want a dedicated venue-demo URL instead, decide it and
   the set re-renders in minutes.
3. **Partner line:** "Founding Limerick Partner programme — by invitation"
   is a marked SPECIMEN. Approve the wording for when the programme opens,
   or cut the line for the first print run.
4. **Screenshots:** v1 uses typographic four-view rows. To run the
   finishing pass with real product screens, a seeded wedding workspace
   needs to exist to shoot (open question in Asset Command). Say the word
   and that becomes the next cycle.
5. Approve for print (≥300gsm uncoated, specs in print-notes.txt) — this is
   the object the outreach email attaches and the founder leaves behind.
