Done 2026-07-16: Hairline chosen, with one amendment (links render indigo). Recorded in content/hq/decisions/email-direction-hairline-2026-07-16.md.

Three email-system directions were implemented and reviewed at
`/hq/email-lab`: Hairline, Broadsheet, Letterhead. Eight representative
emails each, same content, same fixtures. The scorecard
(`docs/email-system/comparison.md`) recommends Hairline (89 to 87 to 84)
for the trust asymmetry; all three remain intact until you choose.

## Why

The code-owned email system is built and validated on branch
`feat/email-design-system`, but converting the suite's mail to one
direction is a taste call only the founder can make.

## While you are there

Nine smaller decisions are queued in
`docs/email-system/decisions-required.md`: the Pro annual price, the Event
price wording, sender addresses, DKIM before any outreach sending, the
school and student sequencing conflict, the student verification method, a
Dispatch email edition, the founder signature line, and a waitlist
confirmation email. None of them block the direction choice.
