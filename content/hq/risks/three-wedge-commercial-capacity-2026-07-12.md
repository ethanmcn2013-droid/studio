## Mitigation

Treat 18 paid venues as the Q1 proof gate. Before scaled spend, productize lead qualification, demo follow-up, contract issue, onboarding, activation, and retention reporting. Once the gate is reached, use revenue to add a commercial or partnerships operator and protect the founder's product and delivery time.

Run each wedge through a separate weekly ledger:

- Venues: contacted, qualified, demos, proposals, paid, activated, renewed.
- Students: qualified visits, checkout conversion, paid, activated, retained.
- Schools: qualified, demos, pilots, paid, activated teachers, retained.

The capacity-backed stretch case of 150 venues, 20,000 students, and 75 schools cannot be used as a committed forecast until the added operator and onboarding system are live.

## Notes

The risk is execution capacity, not addressable demand. A second control applies to School Edition: Year 1 remains teacher-only with zero pupil accounts and zero pupil personal data until a separate compliance decision clears any broader scope.
