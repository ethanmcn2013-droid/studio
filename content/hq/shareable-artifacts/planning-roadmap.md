## Purpose

Give a couple, venue, planner, client, friend, or colleague one clear line that shows how far the project has moved, where today sits, and what comes next.

## Artifact contract

The owner publishes an allowlisted milestone projection and sends its unguessable link. There is no public directory, search surface, or app rail. The recipient sees the same artifact the owner previewed on a phone, while private workspace content stays private.

The view count records qualified publication sessions, not raw requests. This remains Draft until the production migration, release, and live verification receipts exist.
