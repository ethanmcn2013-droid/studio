# Dividend Calculation Framework
## Signal Studio Founder Circle
*Version 1.0 — 2026-05-30*

---

## The Formula

```
GROSS REVENUE
  Less:  Direct costs (hosting, infrastructure, third-party APIs)
         ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  GROSS PROFIT

  Less:  Operating expenses
           Infrastructure and tools
           Professional services (accountancy, legal)
           Marketing and distribution
           Other overhead
         ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  OPERATING PROFIT

  Less:  Corporation tax (Irish rate: 12.5% on trading income)
         ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  POST-TAX PROFIT

  Less:  Operating reserve top-up
           Target: 3 months of operating expenses
           Contribution: 0 when reserve is at target
           ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Less:  Growth investment
           Range: 10–40% of post-tax profit (founder-determined)
           ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  DISTRIBUTABLE PROFIT
```

---

## Distribution Split

Distributable profit is divided proportionally across all shares:

| Holder             | Class   | Share %  | Distribution         |
|--------------------|---------|----------|----------------------|
| [Mam]              | B       | 10.00%   | 10.00% of distributable profit |
| [Friend 1]         | B       | 2.50%    | 2.50%  of distributable profit |
| [Friend 2]         | B       | 2.50%    | 2.50%  of distributable profit |
| [Friend 3]         | B       | 2.50%    | 2.50%  of distributable profit |
| [Friend 4]         | B       | 2.50%    | 2.50%  of distributable profit |
| Ethan McNamara     | A       | 80.00%   | 80.00% of distributable profit |

---

## Quarterly vs. Annual

The calculation is assessed quarterly. If distributable profit exists after a quarter, a distribution may be made.

The decision to distribute is made by the founder at the end of each quarter. If the business is in a strong position and distribution is appropriate, payment is made within 14 working days of the quarter close.

Annual distributions are the sum of four quarterly distributions. There is no annual "true-up" — each quarter stands independently.

*This is an illustrative scenario, not a projection or promise.*


## Year 1 Expectation

In Year 1, the operating reserve is being funded from scratch. Three months of operating expenses (~€9,000 at current cost levels) must be reserved before any distribution is made. It is realistic to expect zero distribution in Year 1.

This is not failure — it is the normal state of a self-funded business building its stability foundation.

## Calculation Record

A simple quarterly calculation record should be maintained at:
`founder-circle/print-assets/calculation-log/YYYY-QN.md`

Each entry records the actual numbers used in that quarter's calculation, whether a distribution was made, and the payment date. This is the basis for any query or audit.

---

## Tax Note for Recipients

Dividend income received by individual shareholders is taxable in Ireland. Recipients are responsible for reporting distributions received to Revenue. The quarterly report will include the gross amount distributed, which recipients should declare on their tax return.

Signal Studio will provide an annual summary of distributions for each recipient by January 31 of the following year.

*This is general information only. Recipients should consult a tax adviser regarding their specific situation.*
