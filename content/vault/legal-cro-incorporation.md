## Phase 4 — Statutory registrations

- [ ] **RBO** (Central Register of Beneficial Ownership): file within 5 months. Beneficial owners = >25% holders → **Ethan (90%) only**; Mam at 10% non-voting is below the threshold and is *not* a beneficial owner *(verify the 25% test covers shares OR voting rights — Ethan is the only one over either)*
- [ ] **Revenue / ROS**: register the company for **Corporation Tax** (and VAT only if/when turnover thresholds require — likely not yet); link via ROS using the CRO number
- [ ] **Business bank account** in the company's name (needs Cert of Incorporation + constitution + ID)
- [ ] Company **seal** (optional but conventional for executing certs/deeds)

---

## Phase 5 — Housekeeping that recurs

- [ ] First **Annual Return (Form B1)** date: CRO assigns an ARD ~6 months after incorporation; the *first* B1 has no financial statements attached, but **do not miss it** — late filing loses audit exemption and incurs penalties
- [ ] Maintain registers as the cap table changes (e.g. if the freelancer equity proceeds, or the Founder Circle expands)
- [ ] Keep the agreement, valuation note, transfer form, and certificate together as the company's foundational record

---

## What you do NOT need

- No authorised-capital ceiling (LTD)
- No memorandum + articles (LTD has a single constitution)
- No §137 bond (EEA-resident director)
- No CAT return expected (tax memo)
- No auditor in year one (audit exemption available for a small company that files on time)
