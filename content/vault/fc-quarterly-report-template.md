# Quarterly Report — Template
## Signal Studio Founder Circle
*Version 1.0 — 2026-05-30*

---

## Structure

Each quarterly report follows the same structure. The format is consistent so recipients know what to expect. The content inside the format is honest and specific to each quarter.

Reports are sent within 14 days of the quarter closing:
- Q1 report: by April 14
- Q2 report: by July 14
- Q3 report: by October 14
- Q4 report: by January 14 (of the following year)

Delivery: via the Founder Circle portal (founders.signalstudio.ie) and, optionally, as a PDF sent by email.

## Annual Report (Q4 Addition)

The Q4 report includes everything above, plus an additional section:

---

**YEAR IN REVIEW · [Year]**

[This section replaces or extends the "Looking ahead" section in Q4. It covers:]

1. **What the year produced** — honest account of revenue, growth, what shipped, what did not.
2. **What changed** — from the start of the year to the end: product, users, understanding.
3. **What was learned** — one or two things that were genuinely surprising or that shifted how Signal Studio is being built.
4. **The year ahead** — the focus for the coming year in 2–3 sentences.

[This section should be 400–600 words. It is the most considered writing of the year. It is worth spending time on.]

---

## Voice Notes for Reports

- The financial section is tabular and precise — numbers are not rounded to a vague range.
- The narrative sections are founder-voice: first person, direct, no hedging.
- Do not use "we" unless referring to actual collaboration with another person. Ethan runs Signal Studio alone. "I" is correct.
- The "What's harder than expected" section must contain something genuine. If every quarter is perfect, the reports become untrustworthy.
- The forward-look is a paragraph, not a bullet list. Bullet lists feel like a pitch deck.
- No images. No charts. Text and numbers only. The design carries through whitespace and typography.
