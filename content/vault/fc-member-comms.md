## 1. Portal Invitation Email

Sent via Clerk on the morning of September 1. The Clerk default invitation email must be replaced with this copy, configured in the Clerk dashboard under "Email Templates → Invitation."

---

**Subject line:**
`Signal Studio · Founder Circle — your portal is live`

**Body:**

---

Signal Studio launched today.

Your portal — where your founder notes, documents, and ownership details will live — is now ready.

[ACCESS YOUR PORTAL]

Your package was posted on [August 26/27/28]. If it has not arrived yet, it will shortly. The letter inside explains everything.

If you have any questions, contact me directly.

Ethan

---

**Formatting notes for Clerk:**
- No logo in the email header — text only
- Plain text or minimal HTML (no coloured backgrounds, no boxes)
- Font: system default — this email is not branded; it is personal
- The [ACCESS YOUR PORTAL] button should link to `founders.signalstudio.ie` with the magic link embedded
- Footer: remove Clerk's default footer if possible; if not, minimise it

**What this email must NOT contain:**
- Marketing language
- "Congratulations"
- Any description of what Signal Studio does
- A list of features or benefits
- "We're excited to..."
- Anything that sounds like an automated onboarding email

## 2. Personal Launch Message (September 1)

Not scripted. These are guidance notes only — the actual message is Ethan's, in his own words.

**For Mam:**

The message should:
- Tell her Signal Studio launched today
- Acknowledge the package is coming or has arrived
- Be warm and brief — nothing performative
- Not repeat the letter — it adds to it, in a different register (this is a text, not a letter)

What it should not do:
- Summarise what Signal Studio is
- Ask for a reaction
- Feel like a notification

**For each friend:**

The message should:
- Reference the package (on its way, or check your post)
- Be direct and human — the same register as how Ethan normally messages this person
- Not be copied and pasted across all four — each one will be slightly different because each person is different

## 3. Portal Launch Note (Day One)

Published in the founder portal on September 1. Visible immediately when a member logs in for the first time. This is not a quarterly report — it is a one-time welcome note.

**Title:** Launch Note · September 1, 2026

**Body:**

---

September 1, 2026

Signal Studio is live today.

This portal is where your founder notes will live — the quarterly updates, your ownership details, your documents. It will grow over time, as the business does.

Your first quarterly report will follow at the end of Q4, in January 2027. For now, everything you need is in the package that was posted to you this week: the certificate, the handbook, and the letter.

If you have questions about anything here — the portal, your shares, or anything else — contact me directly.

Ethan McNamara
Founder, Signal Studio
September 1, 2026

---

**Formatting notes:**
- Displayed in the portal in the same typography as the quarterly reports
- No "read more" — the full note is shown on the home page on Day One
- After Q1 2027, this note is moved to an archive section and the quarterly report takes its place on the home page

## 4. Annual Summary Email

Sent once per year in January, covering the prior year's distributions. A brief covering note accompanies the financial summary.

**Subject:** `Signal Studio · [Year] Annual Distribution Summary`

**Body:**

---

[Year] is done.

Attached is a summary of all Founder Circle distributions for the year — you will need this for your tax return. Your accountant can advise on how to declare dividend income.

The annual section of your Q4 founder note covers what [Year] produced for the business. You will find it in your portal.

Ethan

---

*Attachment: `FC-[Year]-Annual-Distribution-Summary.pdf` — one page per recipient, showing all quarterly distributions received, gross amounts, and payment dates.*

## 6. What the Member Receives on September 1 — Sequence

**Morning (8–10am):**
- Personal message from Ethan (SMS/WhatsApp) arrives
- Portal invitation email arrives in inbox

**Afternoon/evening (or whenever they check the door):**
- Physical package arrives or has already arrived (posted Aug 26–28)

**When they open the portal:**
- They see the Launch Note
- They can access their documents (certificate PDF, letter PDF, handbook PDF)
- Their ownership details are visible
- First quarterly report date is shown

**If the physical package arrives before the portal invite:**
The letter instructs them to expect portal access. If they read the letter first and are curious, they may visit founders.signalstudio.ie — they will see the entry screen and can enter their email. The portal invite they receive later will be expected rather than surprising.

**If the portal invite arrives before the package:**
Normal. They access the portal, see their ownership details and the launch note, and the physical package arrives within a day or two. Nothing breaks. The portal tells them the package is on its way.

## 7. What NOT to Send

- No group messages (all five members together)
- No Signal Studio social post tagging any member
- No email with all members CC'd or BCC'd
- No "announcement" email to a wider list that references the Founder Circle
- No press mention of any member's name or their participation

The Founder Circle is private. It remains private unless a member chooses to mention it themselves.
