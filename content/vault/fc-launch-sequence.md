# September 1 Launch Sequence
## Signal Studio Founder Circle — Master Runbook
*Version 1.0 — 2026-05-30*

---

## The Experience We Are Designing

On September 1, each Founder Circle member experiences this, in roughly this order:

1. A package arrives in the post — or has already arrived in the past day or two
2. They open it and encounter the wax-sealed envelope, their name in Ethan's handwriting
3. They read the letter: *"Signal Studio launched today."*
4. They discover the certificate. They set it somewhere they intend to frame it.
5. They receive an email that morning: portal access
6. They open the portal and find a launch note from Ethan, their ownership details, and the first quarterly report date
7. At some point during the day, they receive a personal message from Ethan — a real one, not scripted

The physical and digital experiences converge on the same day. The letter is the emotional core. The portal is the ongoing relationship. The certificate is what they keep.

---

## The Three Principles of the Launch Sequence

**1. The package leads.** Everything digital is secondary. The portal invite email is not the announcement — the letter is. The email is access to the ongoing relationship.

**2. No surprises after the fact.** Recipients should not be confused about what they have received. The letter explains everything. The handbook explains everything further. The portal makes it permanent.

**3. No fanfare.** This is a private acknowledgement, not a PR moment. No social posts. No group messages. Each person hears from Ethan individually.

## Delivery Risk Management

**If a package is delayed:** The portal invitation still goes out on September 1. The letter says "Signal Studio launched today" — if they receive it on September 2 or 3, the date on the letter still tells the story. The experience is not broken by a one-day delay.

**If a package is lost:** Reprint and re-send. The certificates are not one-of-a-kind prints — the file exists and can be reprinted. The wax seal and handwritten name can be re-applied. Keep one spare of each print item.

**If the portal is not ready:** The physical package stands alone. The portal invite can be delayed by a week without materially affecting the experience. The letter does not reference the portal explicitly.

**If the solicitor work is delayed:** Do not send the certificates until the agreement is executed. If the agreement runs past August 1, post the package with the letter and handbook but hold the certificate — post it separately once the legal instrument is in place. This is not ideal but it is better than sending a certificate that references a legal structure that does not yet formally exist.

## What "September 1" Means

September 1 is the day Signal Studio formally launches. For this project, that means:

- signalstudio.ie is publicly announced or fully live (not just technically live)
- The Founder Circle experience is delivered
- Each member knows the business has started

The specific public announcement (social, press, etc.) is separate from this project and does not affect the Founder Circle timing. The Founder Circle experience is private and personal regardless of how Signal Studio's public launch is communicated.

## The Personal Message (September 1)

Each member receives a personal message from Ethan on September 1. This is not a template — it is a real message written to a real person.

The only guidance:
- It is brief
- It references what day it is
- It says the package is either on its way or should have arrived
- It gives them a way to ask questions if anything is unclear
- It is written in Ethan's own voice — not the handbook voice, not the letter voice — just how he would actually message this person

The message to Mam will be different from the messages to friends in the way that any message to a parent is different. That is for Ethan to write, not to be scripted.
