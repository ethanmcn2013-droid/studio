## 2026-05-14 · reads · The dispatch learns its name

**The suite's shipping log gets a new name and a smaller vocabulary.
It's called the dispatch — what gets sent, not what accumulates.
Five verbs replace the old library-maintainer scaffolding: ships,
tightens, cuts, holds, reads. One of those — holds — is for what
we chose not to build, and why.**

The page reads in plain English, on purpose. No version numbers,
no badges, no jargon. Updated when something is worth saying out
loud. Silence is also brand.
