## 2026-05-15 · ships · A builder's site note joins the worked examples

**The only deep proof of what a Notes note looks like was a wedding
planner's venue note — so a builder or a contractor landing on the
site saw someone else's job. There is now a second worked example at
the same depth: a contractor's tailgate site note.**

A builder walks a job at week six and writes what he found, what he
decided, and the three things still waiting on other people — the
windows, the electrician, the client's tile choice. Real dates,
real money, his own words. The two examples link to each other.
