## 2026-02-25 · ships · Timeline gets a path, not a list

**The second product lands and immediately refuses to be a backlog.
Where most timeline tools render a list of cards stacked vertically,
this one draws a horizontal path — start on the left, the day that
matters on the right, every milestone a station along the way.**

A list says "here are the things we will do, in order." A path says
"here is where we are, and how much further." The 80% asked the
second question. Asking the second question instead of the first is
the only reason this product gets to exist alongside the long list
of timeline tools that already do.
