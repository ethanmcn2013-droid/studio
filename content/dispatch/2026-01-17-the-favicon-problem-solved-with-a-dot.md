## 2026-01-17 · tightens · The favicon problem solved with a dot

**Most small brands lose the brand at the favicon — sixteen square
pixels of compressed colour where the wordmark goes unreadable. The
indigo period solves it on day one. The favicon is the dot. The
shipped icon is the brand at its smallest, not a shrunken version of
something larger.**

Designing the smallest mark first inverts the usual workflow and
catches a lot of bad decisions before they reach the homepage. If a
brand cannot survive sixteen pixels, the brand is doing too much
somewhere else. This one survived sixteen by design.
