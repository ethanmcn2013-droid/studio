## 2026-03-25 · holds · No second colour, no second motion

**Three separate proposals this week to add a second accent colour —
a green for shipped status, a red for blockers, an amber for the
warning state. All three declined. The dot stays one indigo. The
status stays in the word, not in the colour of the word.**

Stoplight palettes are the easiest pattern in product design and the
laziest. They read fine on a designer's screen and read as a
twelve-year-old admin tool the moment a planner opens them on a
phone. The discipline is to make the word do the work.
