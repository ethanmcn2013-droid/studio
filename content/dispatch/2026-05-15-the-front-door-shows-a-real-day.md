## 2026-05-15 · ships · The front door shows a real day, not just a promise

**signalstudio.ie used to open with a line about doing less, then
four product names. The proof it actually works — a real wedding,
planned start to finish — was one click away, and most people never
took it. The home page now shows that day up front.**

One Tuesday, in plain words. The venue call, where forty-five minutes
of notes land in one place. That evening, three of them become the
couple's plan. Midnight, the couple read it on their phone with no
login. Six in the morning, a short briefing names the two things that
need attention — the florist who has gone quiet, the save-the-dates
running late. It is the same wedding the full scene tells, so the
page holds one story instead of asking you to go and find it. The
browser tab says what the work is now, too: project management for
the 80% not in tech.
