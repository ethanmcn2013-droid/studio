## 2026-05-17 · tightens · The pricing page stops promising what it doesn't enforce

**The pricing page said "no feature is gated behind tier." That was
not true, so it is gone. Two things in the suite are part of the
Workspace tier — the morning briefing that arrives by email, and the
forward-to address that turns mail into a note. Every plan can still
read every briefing in the app, and every product is in every tier.
That part held. But the page claimed an absolute it did not keep.**

A pricing page that overpromises is worse than one that says less.
The intro and the closing line now state the shape plainly: all four
products on every plan, two deliveries that come to you instead
riding on Workspace, nothing you make ever locked away by plan. The
gates were already enforced server-side and fail safe to free.
