## 2026-04-08 · ships · Wedding planners get a page that names them

**`/weddings` lands as the first audience-specific landing on the
umbrella. It uses the language a planner uses — venue, florist,
ceremony, run-of-show — not the language a project manager uses,
which is the language every other tool in the category insists on.**

The H1 is "Project management for the 80% not in tech." The page
demonstrates what that sentence means by being a page about weddings
that never says the words workflow, deliverable, stakeholder, or
sprint. Calling the work by its real name is the whole pitch — the
software follows.
