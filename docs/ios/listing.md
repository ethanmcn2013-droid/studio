## Description (4,000)

```
Signal Studio is project management for the 80% who don't work in tech. Four small tools that read as one system — Notes, Tasks, Roadmap, Analytics. Plain English. Built for the work, not the workflow.

What's inside

Notes. A private place for thoughts before they become work. Write it down in three seconds. Decide later what becomes a task.

Tasks. A live workspace built for momentum. Three views of the same list — board, list, timeline. Plain-English dates. Enough motion to show what changed.

Roadmap. Direction clarity. Public plans, decisions, and changes written in plain English. A shareable link, not a PowerPoint.

Analytics. Not a dashboard. A briefing. Reads the state of your work and writes you a short morning note. What needs you. What's moving. What's quiet.

Today

A cross-product home. The day's signal in one place — Tasks waiting on you, the day's briefing, milestones near, notes touched recently. Tap to jump into any product.

Built for wedding planners, freelance designers, tradespeople, students, small-business operators, marketing leads, and anyone running real work in plain English instead of three-adjective grids.

What you won't find

No mascots. No "AI-powered" anything. No three-adjective feature grids. No exclamation marks. Restraint is a feature, not a missing one.

Privacy first

We collect what we need to run the app. Email, your name if you give it, your own content. We don't track you across apps. We don't sell data. We don't have an advertising SDK. Account deletion lives in Settings, irreversible and immediate.

Pricing

Free to start. Workspace plans for individuals and small teams. Student plans free with a .edu email. Event plans for one-time projects like weddings. All pricing on signalstudio.ie/pricing.

Sign in once on your phone, see your work everywhere. signalstudio.ie
```

(2,167 / 4,000 — under target by design; Apple's SERP snippet uses the first ~155 characters and Apple's algorithm rewards descriptions that are dense early and discoverable later)

## Sign in with Apple — gate, not option

App Review 4.8 makes this a **submission requirement, not a nice-to-have**: if any third-party social login button is shown (Google, Microsoft, Facebook, GitHub, etc.), Sign in with Apple must be present alongside it. Clerk's default config exposes Google, so this gates submission.

Before finalising the listing screenshot stack:

- Verify Clerk Dashboard → Social Connections → Apple is enabled for the production instance.
- Verify the `AuthView` (Clerk-iOS UI) in `SignalStudio/Auth/` (Bucket B follow-up) renders Apple alongside Google.
- The sign-in screen screenshot in the App Store stack must show Apple sign-in.
- App Privacy section: no additional disclosures required (Apple's identity provider is not "tracking" by Apple's definition).
