# Venue Creative Production Pack

Status: production work order - 2026-05-27
Owner: founder
Boundary: this pack defines assets for later outreach. No email is sent from this document.

---

## Production Goal

Make the venue motion feel real before it asks for attention.

The first outbound sequence should not rely on a founder explaining the idea in paragraphs. A venue owner or wedding lead should be able to watch 30 seconds, open one page, and understand the mechanic: the venue pays once, each couple gets a code, and the planning year feels calmer under the venue's name.

## 30 Second Proof Video

### Format

| Field | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| Runtime | 30 seconds. Do not exceed 32 seconds. |
| Aspect | 16:9 master. Export 9:16 safe crop only after the master works. |
| Audio | No music. Optional founder VO. Must work muted. |
| Pace | Slow. Six beats, long enough to read. |
| CTA | None. Closing promise and wordmark only. |

### Exact Timeline

| Time | Visual | On-screen text | Audio |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 00:00-00:03 | Paper-white card or still desk surface. | Planning a wedding produces a lot of noise. | Silence or room tone. |
| 00:03-00:06 | Coordinator desk: inbox, notebook, planning notes. Real, not dramatic. | That noise reaches the venue as admin. | Quiet room tone. |
| 00:06-00:10 | Notes product. Venue eyebrow visible. A real wedding note being written. | Notes, where it starts. | Optional VO: "Signal gives every couple one calm place." |
| 00:10-00:14 | Tasks product. Three to five tasks visible; one checked off. | Tasks, where it gets done. | Natural click only. |
| 00:14-00:18 | Roadmap public view. Wide, readable, supplier-safe. | Roadmap, what everyone sees. | Room tone. |
| 00:18-00:22 | Analytics morning briefing. Three plain-English items. | Analytics, what needs attention. | Optional VO: "Silence on the quiet days." |
| 00:22-00:26 | Tight crop on venue eyebrow, then code/redeem mechanic. | Venue pays once. Couple gets a code. Nothing for your team to run. | Founder VO optional. |
| 00:26-00:30 | Closing paper-white card. Signal Studio wordmark below. | Fewer confused couples. Fewer repetitive emails. A better planning experience under your venue's name. | Silence. |

### 30 Second VO

Use only if the no-VO cut feels too abstract. The asset must still work muted.

```text
Planning a wedding produces a lot of noise.

Signal Studio gives every couple one calm place, under your venue's name.

Notes where it starts. Tasks where it gets done. Roadmap what everyone sees. Analytics what needs attention.

The venue pays once. The couple gets a code. There is nothing for your team to run.

Fewer confused couples. Fewer repetitive emails. A better planning experience under your venue's name.
```

## One-Page PDF Layout

### Format

| Field | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| Size | A4 portrait. |
| Density | One page only. No overflow page. |
| Visual | One strong product frame, preferably the venue eyebrow tight crop. |
| CTA | Footer line only: "Worth a short venue conversation?" |
| Export | `signal-studio-venue-edition-one-page.pdf` |

### Layout

| Zone | Copy | Design note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Top | Signal Studio Venue Edition. The planning layer premium venues give every couple after booking. | Quiet wordmark, plenty of white space. |
| Lead | A couple books your venue. Everyone is warm. Then the planning year starts. Questions already answered. Supplier details scattered. Spreadsheet versions that stopped being current. That confusion reaches your team as admin. | Keep as one compact paragraph. |
| Product visual | Screenshot with venue eyebrow visible. | The visual must prove "under your venue's name" without a logo wall. |
| Four layers | Notes where it starts. Tasks where it gets done. Roadmap what everyone sees. Analytics what needs attention. | Four short rows, not cards. |
| Mechanic | The venue pays once a year. Each couple gets a code. The couple gets twelve months of Signal Studio. Your team has nothing to run. | Make this the cleanest part of the page. |
| Pricing | Founding venues lock EUR1,500/year while the first cohort is open. Standard venue tiers are EUR1,500-EUR4,000/year. | Plain. Do not hide price. |
| Close | Fewer confused couples. Fewer repetitive emails. A better planning experience under your venue's name. | Same as video close. |

## Quality Gate

| Gate | Pass condition |
| --- | --- |
| Premium restraint | The piece feels like Signal: quiet, exact, and useful. |
| Venue respect | It never implies the product replaces the coordinator or wedding manager. |
| Buyer/user mechanic | The venue pays; the couple uses; the venue has nothing to run. |
| Product truth | Every product frame is a real or plausible shipped surface, not a mock fantasy. |
| Mobile legibility | The closing card, product names, and venue eyebrow survive mobile viewing. |
| No early CTA | No "book a demo" language appears in the video or PDF. |
