## Method

This register separates published market evidence from Signal Studio commercial assumptions. Market totals do not equal qualified pipeline. Counts use the latest final official releases found during the July 2026 review and disclose mixed reference years where national publication cycles differ.

## Privacy boundary for School Edition

School Edition is a teacher-only commercial hypothesis. Year 1 permits verified adult staff accounts only. It excludes pupil accounts, pupil personal data, assessment data, and classroom claims.

Primary guidance:

- UK Department for Education, procuring education technology: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/data-protection-in-schools/procuring-educational-technology-edtech
- Data Protection Commission, Fundamentals for a Child-Oriented Approach to Data Processing: https://www.dataprotection.ie/en/dpc-guidance/blogs/the-children-fundamentals
