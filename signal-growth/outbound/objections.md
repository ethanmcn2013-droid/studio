# Objections

## I Already Use Spreadsheets

Response: keep the spreadsheet where it helps. Use Signal Studio for ownership, follow-up, decisions, and what changed.

## I Do Not Want Another Tool

Response: Signal Studio should replace the scattered middle, not add a new layer.

## My Clients Will Not Use Software

Response: they should be able to open a shared page and understand it in under 60 seconds.

## We Already Have A Planning System

Response: Signal Studio can start as the plain-language layer for guests, clients, couples, suppliers, or people who do not live in the internal system.
