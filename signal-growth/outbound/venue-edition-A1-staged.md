> Hello,
>
> I run a small Irish software studio called Signal Studio. I design it myself, in Dublin.
>
> Couples don't choose Clontarf Castle for a function room — they choose the Great Hall under the Battle of Clontarf frieze, the building itself, and then spend the year coordinating everything else over email.
