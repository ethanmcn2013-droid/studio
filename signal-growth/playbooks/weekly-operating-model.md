# Weekly Operating Model

## Monday

- Build prospect list.
- Publish one founder or product post.

## Tuesday

- Send 20 personalised outreach emails.

## Wednesday

- Record or edit one demo clip.

## Thursday

- Follow up with prospects.
- Speak to users.

## Friday

- Review metrics.
- Identify objections and feedback themes.

## Weekend

- Improve one landing page, template, or demo based on feedback.
