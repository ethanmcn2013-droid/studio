# Voice And Tone

Signal Studio speaks in plain language.

Use:

- clear work
- plain English
- briefing
- attention
- context
- direction
- execution
- decisions
- what changed
- what needs attention

Avoid:

- hype
- jargon
- fake urgency
- vague productivity promises
- dashboard-heavy language
- claims that the product does not prove yet

Marketing should feel calm, useful, and specific. One good idea can become many assets, but every reuse should still read like it was made on purpose.
