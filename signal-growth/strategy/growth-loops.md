# Growth Loops

## Core Product Loop

Workspace created -> collaborators invited -> work becomes clearer -> shareable outputs created -> new people discover Signal Studio -> some become workspace creators -> more workspaces created.

## Early Wedge

Weddings and events are the first validation wedge.

Why:

- deadline-bound work
- many people involved
- visible planning artefacts
- clear partner channels
- strong need for updates, decisions, and follow-up

Ireland is the validation market, not the ceiling.

## Product-Led Surfaces

- shared roadmap pages
- public template pages
- shareable updates
- decision summaries
- workspace briefings
- demo workspaces
- partner pilot pages
