Signal Studio - identity & stationery print notes (v2, deck grammar)
=====================================================================

All print PDFs: vector RGB, 3mm bleed + crop marks. Convert to PDF/X-1a at
prepress. Dark objects (founder card front, cafe card, K3 card, A2 poster)
use #0a0a0b panels - ask prepress for a RICH BLACK build (e.g. 60/40/40/100),
not plain 100K, so the panel prints deep and even on uncoated stock.

founder-card-front-print.pdf / founder-card-back-print.pdf
  85x55mm - ink-dark front over white reverse (the deck's card system).
  Stock: 350-400gsm uncoated, duplex. founder-card-front-light-print.pdf
  is the paper-front alternate, kept for comparison.
fp-card-print.pdf
  The Founding Partner variant - solid indigo (PMS 2726C is the closest
  spot match to #4f46e5 if offset), numbered at print or by hand, never
  reprinted. Presented at signing only.
cafe-card-print.pdf          A6 dark - permission placement only.
campaign-poster-print.pdf    A2 (420x594mm) dark - permission boards only.
letterhead-print.pdf         A4 white, 100-120gsm uncoated.

Before print: QR destinations confirmed, location line (Limerick) confirmed,
founder sign-off (gate check 10). SPECIMEN-marked objects never print.
