Signal Studio — identity & stationery print notes
==================================================

All PDFs are vector RGB (Chromium render). Ask the printer to convert to
PDF/X-1a at prepress. Text is pure black (100K after conversion); the only
colour is brand indigo #4f46e5 (front-of-card period, headline full stops).
Crop marks and 3mm bleed are included on every *-print.pdf.

founder-card-front-print.pdf / founder-card-back-print.pdf
  Trim 85×55mm · 3mm bleed · safe margin 6mm
  Stock: 350–400gsm uncoated (duplex for weight if available) · no lamination, no gloss
  Front and back supplied as separate artboards per printer convention.

cafe-card-print.pdf
  Trim 105×148mm (A6 portrait) · 3mm bleed · safe margin 10mm
  Stock: ≥300gsm uncoated · placement is permission-led only, never stacked or dropped.

letterhead-print.pdf
  Trim 210×297mm (A4) · 3mm bleed · 18mm document margins
  Stock: 100–120gsm uncoated. letterhead-screen.pdf is the emailable version;
  the HTML master (collateral repo, masters/identity/letterhead.html) is the
  editable source — set letter text inside .letter-body.

signature.html / signature.txt
  Copy the block between SIGNATURE START/END into the mail client.
  Fonts fall back Geist → Helvetica Neue → Arial; no images, nothing to break.

Before print (founder confirmations)
  1. Location line reads "Limerick, Ireland" — the previous live signature said
     Dublin. Confirm Limerick is correct everywhere.
  2. Role title "Founder" and name spelling confirmed as printed.
  3. Founder sign-off (quality gate check 10) before any print run.
