The Problem
Coordination failures cost ~€2,400 per worker each year —
80% still run projects on WhatsApp and spreadsheets.

Project-management tools were designed for engineering teams — sprint boards, story points, Gantt charts. Venue coordinators, wedding planners, tradespeople, and freelancers are handed the same tools and expected to adapt.

The result: coordination by WhatsApp, spreadsheet, and repeated email. Work gets lost. Status is unclear. The person responsible for the outcome cannot see what is done, pending, or urgent — in one place.

This is operational friction with a real weekly cost — most acute in high-stakes, multi-party projects like weddings and events.
